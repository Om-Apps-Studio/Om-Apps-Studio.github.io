Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 14, 2024

1. Introduction

FieldCam ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application FieldCam (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, do not download, install, or use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • User Information: Person names, notes, and other text you enter in the App
  • Settings: Your preferences, categories, templates, and configuration settings
  • Department Logo: Images you select for use as department logos
  • Sync Credentials: FTP server credentials and Google account information (stored locally on your device)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Data: GPS coordinates when you capture photos or videos (if location services are enabled)
  • Media Files: Photos and videos you capture using the App
  • Device Information: Device model, Android version, and technical information necessary for App functionality
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the App (stored locally)

2.3 Information from Third-Party Services

  • Google Drive: When you enable Google Drive sync, we access your Google Drive account to upload media files. This requires your explicit permission.
  • NTP Servers: We connect to Network Time Protocol servers to synchronize time. No personal information is transmitted.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To provide core features including photo/video capture, metadata stamping, and media organization
  • Cloud Synchronization: To upload your media files to Google Drive or FTP servers when you enable sync features
  • Time Synchronization: To synchronize device time with NTP servers for accurate timestamps
  • Location Services: To embed GPS coordinates in your media files when location services are enabled
  • Settings Persistence: To remember your preferences and settings across app sessions
  • Error Reporting: To identify and fix bugs (error logs stored locally)

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All data collected by the App is stored locally on your device:

  • Media Files: Stored in your device's standard photo/video storage locations
  • Database: App settings and metadata stored in a local SQLite database
  • Preferences: User preferences stored using Android DataStore
  • Credentials: FTP and sync credentials stored securely using Android's encrypted storage

4.2 Cloud Storage

When you enable cloud sync features:

  • Google Drive: Media files are uploaded to your Google Drive account. We only access folders/files created by the App.
  • FTP Servers: Media files are uploaded to FTP servers you configure. We do not have access to your FTP credentials beyond what's necessary for file transfer.

4.3 Security Measures

  • All data stored locally on your device
  • No data transmitted to our servers (except NTP time sync, which contains no personal information)
  • FTP credentials encrypted using Android's secure storage
  • Google Drive access uses OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Cloud Sync: When you enable sync, media files are uploaded to your configured cloud storage (Google Drive or FTP)
  • Sharing Features: When you use the App's share functionality, files are shared through Android's standard sharing mechanism
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or in response to valid legal process

Important: We do not have access to your Google Drive or FTP server data beyond what you explicitly authorize.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Drive API: For cloud storage synchronization. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • NTP Servers: For time synchronization. No personal data is transmitted.
  • Android System Services: Camera, location, storage, and other system services as required for App functionality.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: You can access all data stored by the App through the App's interface
  • Deletion: You can delete media files, settings, and data through the App or by uninstalling the App
  • Location Services: You can disable location services at any time through device settings
  • Cloud Sync: You can disable cloud sync features at any time in App settings
  • Camera Permissions: You can revoke camera permissions through device settings (App will not function without camera permission)
  • Storage Permissions: You can manage storage permissions through device settings

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete such information.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the App's functionality:

  • Media Files: Retained until you delete them through the App or device file manager
  • Settings: Retained until you change or delete them, or uninstall the App
  • Sync Logs: Retained locally for troubleshooting purposes

When you uninstall the App, all locally stored data is deleted. Cloud-synced files remain in your cloud storage according to your cloud provider's retention policies.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored locally on your device. When you enable cloud sync:

  • Google Drive files are stored according to Google's data location policies
  • FTP server files are stored on servers you configure
  • We do not transfer your data to our servers

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy
  • Including a notice in the App (for significant changes)

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes are effective when posted on this page.

12. Open Source

FieldCam is an open-source application. The source code is available on GitHub, allowing you to review exactly how your data is handled. We encourage security researchers and privacy advocates to review our code and report any concerns.
